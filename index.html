COS Guide
0650hrs
1. Reach NSDC by 0650hrs
2. Draw "34FMD HQ Office Key" - Key 07 from 6AMB Ops Room.
3. Unlock DHQ Office by 0700hrs
0700hrs
1. Retrieve Key C02 and open the COS Drawer
2. Ensure you have the following items in the 3rd drawer:
  1. Key Register Book
  2. COS Hoto Book
  3. 34FMD Photocopy Logbook
  4. 34FMD COS Logbook
  5. 34FMD Attendance File
  6. 34FMD RSO Medical Certificate File
  7. 34FMD Visitors Logbook
3. Place the following items on your table, the rest can go back in the drawer:
  1. Key Register Book
  2. COS Hoto Book
  3. 34FMD Photocopy Logbook
  4. 34FMD Attendance File
4. Open the Key Register Book and go to the latest new page. Sign for the C02 key which you have drawn earlier.
5. Check with DHQ personnel whether DC is present. If DC is present, help to sign DC office key in the key register book.
6. Retrieve a HOTO Checklist from the HOTO file. Adhere to the checklist throughout the day.
7. Prepare your Short COS Template.
0730hrs
1. Send the Short COS Template you prepared earlier into the 34FMD groupchat.
2. Send the Greeting Template immeidately after sending the Short COS Template
3. Text each POC of the CSWs to ask if any contractors have RSOed.
Throughout the day(Receiving MCs/Statuses)
1. Throughout the day, people will update you on the statuses they received. Update the Short COS Template accordingly, and update the Greeting Template accordingly as well.
2. (MC <3 Days) When a serviceman receives any amount of MC, they are to apply for it on ESS and take a screenshot of it. They are to fill up the S1 Br Reporting Template. Redirect them to this link to generate it and send it over to you. https://3ambs1brrishi.github.io/S1-BR-MC-Reporting.io/
3. (MC <3 Days) Send the ESS Screenshot and the message together in 1 single message to the 34FMD groupchat.
4. (MC >3 Days) When a serviceman receives >3(Not 3 or <3 days.) days of MC or Hospitalisation Leave. You are to inform Ops Room. Get the serviceman to do up the Ops Room Reporting Template Generator and send it to you. https://opstest1.netlify.app/
5. (MC >3 Days) Send the Short COS Template, Updated Greeting Template, S1 Br Template, Ops Room Reporting Template and the updated Greeting Template. You are to ask for permission before informing Ops Room.
Throughout the day(Photocopying)
1. People may approach you to photocopy documents. As a COS, you are NOT ALLOWED to photocopy anything. ALWAYS approach DHQ personnel to hep photocopy documents. Get the recipient to sign the Photocopy Logbook.
Throughout the day(Personnel Movement)
1. If someone is going on an MA/RSI after coming to camp, you are required to update the COS Short Template and send the personnel movement template as well. Get the personnel to update you constantly on when they have reached/left the location.
1630-1700hrs - Near the End of the Day
1. Begin crafting the long End-Of-Day Template. The long template should include the overview tab and multiple other tabs that you should fill in based on all the new details/statuses you have received for the day.
2. Ensure that the orderly has thrown the trash in DHQ.(Not just the toilet)
1730hrs - End of the day
1. Ensure all electrical appliances/outlets are switched off.
2. Wait for all keys to be returned before closing the following books:
  1. Keypress Book
  2. Photocopy Logbook
  3. HOTO Checklist
3. Sign the COS Hoto checklist under “Handing Over”
4. Wait for the Duty Sergeant to sign off the 3 documents.
5. Ensure the COS table is tidy, no kitchenware in sight.
6. Return all documents to the COS Drawer(3rd Drawer).
7. Ensure that the protection bar of the drawer is in place and locked.
8. Ensure that the power plug of the COS OSN/laptop is on and plugged into the OSN.
9. Turn off lights and aircon.
10. Lock DHQ Office and ensure all rooms in DHQ are locked.
11. Return the key to 6AMB Ops Room
12. Send the final LONG COS template
13. Pat yourself on the back, knowing you are 1 day closer to ORD.
